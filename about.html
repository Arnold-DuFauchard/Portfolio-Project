About Me

I’m a collector of experiences. My favorite things are going on adventures with my friends and family, including my Saint Bernard rescue pup Luna. I love to capture the joy of these memories and make them last for generations to come. There’s no better time to capture your family photos then when you’re having fun! I started out as an equine photographer and gradually incorporated more humans and portraiture, however photographing equines still holds a special place in my heart! I emphasize a natural style with mild editing to bring out the innate beauty of the setting and subject. I enjoy being outside and appreciating all of life's magnificent features - which are sometimes the best props! My style focuses on using natural lights to the fullest advantage so your beautiful pictures are more natural and elegant. Schedule a session with me to make your precious family memories last a lifetime!
